## This repository contains a dapp who integrated web3-onboard with a single component builted. 

Web3-Onboard is the quickest and easiest way to add multi-wallet and multi-chain support to your project. With built-in modules for more than 35 unique hardware and software wallets, Web3-Onboard saves you time and headaches.

it was builded attending BUILDH3R | Interactive Learn & Earn Workshops

This was provided by Laurence Delisle , Sr PMM at Blocknative. Thank you for your time and efforts.
